Beef Stew

Description

A traditional braised beef casserole with thick, rich gravy and chunky veg â€“ an ideal recipe for cheap cuts, as slow-cooking guarantees a tender dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 celery sticks, thickly sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 large carrots, chunkily sliced
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 850g stewing beef, large chunks

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 140C fan and put the kettle on.
  2. Put celery, onion, carrots, bay leaves and one whole thyme sprig in a flameproof dish with the oil and butter.
  3. Soften for 10 minutes, then stir in flour until it no longer looks dusty. Then add the tomato puree, Worcestershire sauce and the stock cubes.
  4. Gradually stir in 600ml of hot water, then tip in the beef and bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and put int he oven for 2hrs 30 mins, then uncover and cook for a further 30mins - 1hr until the meat is tender and the sauce thickened.
  6. Garnish with the remaining thyme.
